Purpose

The purpose of Turkana TV in the community is to serve as a platform for local news, information, and entertainment that is relevant and accessible to the people of Turkana. It is to promote and celebrate the culture and diversity of the region, while also providing a platform for discussion and debate on important issues affecting the community. Additionally, Turkana TV is to strive to empower the local population by providing opportunities for training and employment in the media industry. By doing so, it is to help to promote economic growth and development in the region. Overall, the purpose of Turkana TV is to serve as a trusted and reliable source of information, entertainment, and education for the people of Turkana, while also contributing to the growth and development of the community as a whole.

Audience

The audience for Turkana TV would primarily be the people of Turkana, including those living in urban and rural areas. This would include a diverse range of individuals, such as farmers, traders, pastoralists, students, teachers, business owners, and government officials. Turkana TV may also attract viewers from other parts of Kenya or even from other countries who have an interest in the culture, history, and current affairs of Turkana. Additionally, the channel may also appeal to members of the Kenyan diaspora who want to stay connected to their roots and keep up to date with events and developments in their home region. Overall, the audience for Turkana TV would be a mix of local, national, and international viewers who have an interest in the people, culture, and events of Turkana.
